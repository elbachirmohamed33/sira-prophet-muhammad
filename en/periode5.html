The Night of Destiny

Date: 27 Ramadan 610 CE (40 years old)

Place: Cave of Hirâ' (Mount Nour)

What he experienced before the revelation:

  • Spent entire nights meditating
  • Frequently dreamed of truths that came true
  • Hated the idolatrous practices of his people

The moving scene:

  • The angel Jibrîl suddenly appeared in human form
  • Embraced him strongly three times
  • Ordered him "Read!" although he could not read
  • The first verses emerged like a light

His human reaction:

  • Ran home trembling, asked Khadija: "Cover me!"
  • Feared he had become a poet or possessed
  • Confided in his cousin Waraqa: "I fear for my life"

Why it speaks to us: This scene shows that even God's great chosen ones know fear and doubt. Revelation is a burden before it is an honor.

Khadija, First Refuge

Date: June 610 CE

Place: Their home in Makkah

Her unconditional support:

  • Wrapped him in a cloak to calm him
  • First to believe in him: "Allah will never abandon you"
  • Consulted her Christian cousin Waraqa to reassure him

Touching words: "I would be ready to die for you" (reported by 'Aisha)

Lesson of love: Behind every great man is an exceptional woman. The Prophet mourned her memory until his own death.
