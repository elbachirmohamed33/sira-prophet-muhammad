Refuge with the Negus

Date: 615 CE

Place: Royal court of Aksum

The perilous journey:

  • 83 Muslims including 18 women cross the Red Sea
  • Ja'far ibn Abî Tâbil carries the Quran sewn into his garment

Historic scene:

  • Before the Christian king, Ja'far recites Surah Maryam (19:16-33)
  • The Negus weeps: "These words and those of Jesus come from the same source"
  • He refuses to hand over the refugees despite bribes from the Quraysh

Current lesson: First example of diplomatic protection of religious minorities.
