An Illuminated Birth

Date: 12 Rabi al-Awwal 570 CE

Place: House of his uncle Abû Tâlib (Valley of Banû Hâshim)

The sacred moment:

  • Born at dawn, while the palace of Kisra (Persians) trembled
  • His mother Âmina heard a voice: "You have given birth to the master of this community"
  • First words: "Allahu Akbar Kabîran" (Musnad Ahmad)

A different child:

  • From birth, he prostrated placing his hands on the ground (Sîra d'Ibn Ishâq)
  • Refused to suckle on Fridays out of respect
  • Never cried like other babies

Why it matters: This birth marks the arrival of the Mercy promised in the Torah and Gospel. A hope for all humanity.

Childhood in the Desert

Date: 575-578 CE

Place: Camp of Banû Sa'd (arid region south of Mecca)

With Halima, the nurse:

  • Poor family who took him out of necessity
  • Miracle: Their lame donkey became the fastest
  • Their sheep gave abundant milk

The moving episode:

  • At 4 years old, two angels opened his chest:
  • They washed his heart with pure water
  • Removed a black drop (trace of Shaytân)
  • Sealed his side with the ring of prophecy (Sahih Muslim)

The heartbreak: Halima, terrified, returned him to his mother crying: "Take your child, I fear harm may come to him"

What it teaches us: Allah prepared His Prophet to carry the heaviest message, while preserving his innate purity.
