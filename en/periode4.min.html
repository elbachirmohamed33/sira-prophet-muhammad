Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

The Lone Shepherd

Age: 8-12 years

Place: Hills around Mecca

His days:

  • Shepherded sheep for a few dirhams
  • Observed stars and meditated
  • Avoided vulgar games of other children

Touching words: "All prophets tended flocks. I too grazed my sheep near Ajya." (Bukhârî)

The Journey That Changed Everything

Age: 12 years (582 CE)

Route: Mecca → Bosra (Syria)

With his uncle Abû Tâlib: First commercial experience

Meeting with the monk Bahîra: "He is the Lord of the Worlds whose coming I read about in our books"

His reflections:

  • Horrified by idolatry in Petra
  • Fascinated by Syriac churches
  • Returned determined to find the Truth

Did you know? It was during this journey that he first saw chained slaves - an image that marked his future fight against oppression.
