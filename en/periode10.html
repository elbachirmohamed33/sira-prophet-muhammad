The Assassination Plot

Date: 26 Safar 622 CE | Moonless night

Place: Prophet's house in Makkah

Chilling details:

  • 11 Qurayshite chiefs (including Abû Jahl and Ubayy) draw lots to stab him
  • Each tribe must provide a youth to strike together → Escape from pursuers

Divine miracle:

  • The Prophet ﷺ throws dust on them while reciting Quran 36:9 → Temporarily blinded
  • 'Alî sleeps in his bed with his sheepskin → Daggers pierce the fabric without touching him

The Cave of Thawr

Date: 3 days hidden (27-29 Safar)

Poignant scenes:

  • Abû Bakr cries seeing a snake → The Prophet calms him: "Do not grieve, Allah is with us"
  • A spider weaves its web in 1 hour → Miracle confirmed by biologists (ultra-strong silk)
  • Pigeons nest at the entrance → Eggs hatch in 24 hours (naturally impossible)

Miraculous food:

  • Asmâ' brings provisions → Food multiplies
  • A shepherd (on Abû Bakr's order) brings fresh milk every night
