The Double Loss

Date: Ramadan 619 CE

Death of Abû Tâlib:

  • Last poignant dialogue: "Uncle, say 'Lâ ilâha illâllah' for your salvation..."
  • "I would if I did not fear the Quraysh's reproach"

Death of Khadija:

  • Buried in a shroud woven by her own hands
  • The Prophet himself dug her grave at Hujûn

Consequences:

  • The Quraysh throw entrails on him during his prayer
  • Surviving children: Fâtima (9 years old) becomes his consolation

Revealing words: "This year left me alone and wounded."
