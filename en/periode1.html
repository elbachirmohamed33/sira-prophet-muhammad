Creation of His Light (Nûr Muhammadiyya)

Date: Unknown (before the creation of Adam)

Place: Pre-created world (ʿĀlam al-Dharr)

Key Figures: ALLAH, Angels

Sources:

  • Weak Hadith in Bayhaqî (Dalâ'il an-Nubuwwa 1/24)
  • Tafsîr al-Qurtubî (18/301) mentioning the tradition
  • Poetry of al-Busayrî (Qasîdat al-Burda)

Summary: Allah created the prophetic light (Nûr Muhammadiyya) before any material existence. According to Tafsîr al-Alûsî (Rûh al-Ma'ânî), this light was placed in the lineage of Adam up to ʿAbdullâh.

Main Lesson: The prophecy of Muhammad ﷺ is the cosmic pivot of creation, as confirmed by the Quran (21:107): "We have sent you only as a mercy to the worlds."

Alliance of the Prophets (Mîthâq)

Date: Before the creation of the heavens and the earth

Place: Celestial kingdom (ʿĀlam al-Malakût)

Key Figures: All prophets (Ibrâhîm, Mûsâ, ʿÎsâ), Witnessing angels

Sources:

  • Quran 3:81: "And when Allah took the covenant of the prophets..."
  • Tafsîr Ibn Kathîr (2/32) with chain of transmission
  • Sahîh Muslim (hadith 2365) on prophetic primacy

Summary: Allah made the 124,000 prophets (according to Ibn Hibban) swear to believe in Muhammad ﷺ if he came during their lifetime. This pact is mentioned in Tafsîr al-Tabarî (3/171).

Main Lesson: Islam is the culmination of all previous revelations, as confirmed by the Quran (5:3): "Today I have perfected for you your religion."
