Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

Chapter: Conquest of Mecca (8H/630 CE)

Home Previous Chapter Next Chapter

Victory Without Bloodshed

Secret Preparations:

  • 4 converging columns (10,000 men)
  • Fires lit at night to simulate a giant army
  • Double agents (like Abû Sufyân) neutralized
  • Strict orders: "Fight only those who resist", "Spare anyone who seeks refuge with Abû Sufyân"

5-Act Sequence:

  • Peaceful entry: Khalid ibn Walid briefly confronts Ikrima's archers, only 12 deaths (including 2 Muslims)
  • Purification of the Kaaba: Destruction of 360 idols with a stick while reciting Quran 17:81
  • General pardon: Blacklist of 10 criminals pardoned except 4 (executed for previous murders), Wahshi (Hamza's killer) pardoned after conversion
  • First sermon: "O Quraysh, what do you think I will do to you today?", Trembling response: "You are a noble brother, son of a noble brother...", "I say to you as Yusuf said to his brothers: No blame on you today! (Quran 12:92), Go, you are free."
  • Reorganization: Bilâl calls to prayer on the Kaaba roof, Keys given to Banû Shayba (historical guardians)

Human & Spiritual Outcome:

  • Duration: 3 days
  • Deaths: 28 (including 6 Muslims)
  • Conversions: 2,000 in 24 hours
  • Prisoners: 0
  • Booty: Voluntarily renounced

Geopolitical Impact: All Arab tribes send delegations, End of polytheism in Arabia in 18 months
