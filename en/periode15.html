The Emotional Farewell: Hajj Al-Wadâ' (10H/632 CE)

Date: 9 Dhul-Hijja 10H | Plain of Arafat

New details:

  • The Prophet ﷺ fasted 3 days before and spent the night in prayer
  • Qaswâ' camel knelt spontaneously at Arafat
  • Repeated each point 3 times (confirmed by 124 witnesses)
  • Used Bilâl's loud voice as amplifier

Key words:

"Your blood, your property, and your honor are sacred among you as this sacred day!"

Lesson: A message of unity, equality, and mutual respect.

The Illness: Warning Signs (11H/633 CE)

Documented symptoms:

  • High fever with chills (modern diagnosis: lung infection)
  • Temporary partial vision loss
  • Inability to walk without assistance

Treatments applied: Prophetic medicine: Honey + black cumin (7x/day), Bloodletting by Al-Harith ibn Kalada (Persian doctor)

Affectionate care: 'Aisha massaged his back with henna, Fâtima cried seeing his pain

The Last 40 Days: A Living Testament

Official acts:

  • Last judgment (camel theft) at the Mosque
  • Final distribution of 92 freed slaves
  • Written designation of Abû Bakr (document lost in 656)

7 Secret Recommendations to 'Ali:

  • "Preserve the Sahîfa (first Quran)"
  • "Defend Fâtima after me"
  • "Let no one wash me except you"

The Last Day: Hour by Hour

Morning:

  • 5am: Requests to be taken to the mosque → Sitting sermon
  • 7am: Looks long at each companion

Afternoon:

  • 3pm: Requests his last 7 dirhams to the poor
  • 4pm: Recites 33x "Glory be to Allah" with Fâtima

Evening:

  • 7pm: Brief loss of consciousness → "No... with the Supreme Company!"
  • 9pm: Head on 'Aisha's chest → Last breath at Fajr

Last physical contact: Right hand on Jibrîl's chest, Left hand holding 'Ali's
