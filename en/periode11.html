The Construction of Quba

Date: Only 4 days (8 to 12 Rabi' al-Awwal)

Physical participation: The Prophet himself carried bricks marked with his fingerprints

Work chant: "This burden is nothing compared to that of the hereafter"

Social innovations: First minbar: Tree stump as pulpit, Night school, Public toilets dug apart

Brotherhood (al-Mu'âkhât)

90 pairs united

Touching examples:

  • Abû Bakr shares half of his wealth with Khârijah ibn Zayd
  • Sa'd ibn Rabî' offers half of his house, orchard, and wife to 'Abd al-Rahmân ibn 'Awf (who politely refuses)

Golden rule: "No believer sleeps full while his brother is hungry"
