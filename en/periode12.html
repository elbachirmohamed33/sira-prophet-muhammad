Historical Context

Date: Month of Dhul-Qi'dah

Neutral zone: 20 km from Mecca

Participants: Muslims: 1,400 unarmed pilgrims (in Ihram), Quraysh: 200 horsemen led by Suhayl ibn Amr

Claims: Right to perform 'Umrah this year, Release of Muslim prisoners

Treaty terms (Full version):

  • 10-year truce
  • Immediate return to Medina (pilgrimage postponed)
  • Controversial clause: Any Meccan joining Medina will be returned, Any Medinite returning to Mecca will not be returned
