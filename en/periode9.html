Preparation for the Journey

Date: Night of 27 Rajab

Place: Al-Aqsa Mosque (Jerusalem)

Spiritual purification:

  • Angels wash his heart with Zamzam water
  • Receives 3 cups: milk (wisdom), wine (temptation), honey (prophecy)
  • Symbolic choice: "I choose the milk" (Sahih al-Bukhari 3207)

Extraordinary mount:

  • Al-Burâq: "Bigger than a donkey, smaller than a mule"
  • "Its stride reached the horizon" (Ibn Kathîr)
  • Refused to move until Muhammad ﷺ stroked it

The Seven Heavens (New Details)

1st Heaven: World of Clouds

  • Meeting: Adam عليه السلام
  • Dialogue: "Here is your father Adam, greet him" (Jibrîl)
  • "Welcome my pious son and virtuous prophet" (Adam)
  • Phenomenon: Souls of martyrs floating like green birds

2nd Heaven: World of Stars

  • Meeting: Jesus (ʿÎsâ) and John the Baptist (Yaḥyâ)
  • Position: Side by side like brothers (Sahih Muslim 162)
  • Revealed secret: "We are cousins through my mother Mary"

3rd Heaven: Garden of Delights

  • Meeting: Joseph (Yûsuf)
  • Appearance: "Perfect beauty like the full moon" (Tirmidhî)
  • Symbol: Golden key (divine wisdom) in his hand

4th Heaven: World of Light

  • Meeting: Idrîs
  • Revelation: "I was raised alive to the heavens" (Quran 19:57)
  • Special gift: Book of longevity written in letters of fire

5th Heaven: World of Iron

  • Meeting: Aaron (Hârûn)
  • Attribute: Holding the staff that defeated Pharaoh
  • Warning: "Your community will experience divisions like mine"

6th Heaven: World of Pearls

  • Meeting: Moses (Mûsâ)
  • Touching exchange: "How many prayers has Allah prescribed?" "50 per day" → "Return to ask for reduction" (x5)

7th Heaven: Sidrat al-Muntahâ

  • Phenomena: Tree covered with angels, Rivers of light, Divine Encounter
  • Final prescription: 5 daily prayers
